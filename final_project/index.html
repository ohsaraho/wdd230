About Us

Our purpose is to provide a bridge between all the local businesses so that we can all work together to grow our business and serve our community. Hoping to deliver service that will provide help for business who are already members of this commerce and are interested in joining.

Our goals are to provide the local businesses and community with fun events to tend to as well as some learning events through out the year that teach on skills to improve your business. Providing contacts for businesses to get in touch with other businesses and so forth.

We hope that every event we put on that each and everyone of you who intend can feel the team work atmosphere that we try to strive for with serving our community.
