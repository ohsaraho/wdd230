Salt Lake City, UT

Historical Information

Salt Lake City was founded by a group of Mormon pioneers on July 24,1847. They came to Salt Lake City in search to be able to practice their religion so they could be free of persecution.

When the Mormons first arrived the region was part of Mexico but later in 1848 a treaty was signed that said it belonged to the United States. The "State of Deseret" became the Utah Territory later in 1850 which Deseret means honeybee. Our state symbol is a beehive representing successful industry that we can help create together.
